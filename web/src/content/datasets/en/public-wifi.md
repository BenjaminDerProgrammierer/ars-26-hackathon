The WGS84 location table includes recognizable matches such as AEC Dach,
Brucknerhaus, and Hauptplatz. A separate [usage dataset](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/d849807f-d313-45fb-a7b6-af3f726a1673)
contains monthly counts for January–July 2022.

Use the locations only as a visibly dated supporting layer. Neither the points
nor the historical usage figures establish 2026 connectivity, capacity,
availability, or crowd levels.
