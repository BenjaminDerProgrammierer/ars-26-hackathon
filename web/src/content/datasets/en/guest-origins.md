The annual tables report arrivals and overnight stays by residence country or
region and quarter. They can be joined through a reviewed German-label-to-ISO
crosswalk to the 238 festival contacts that currently carry a country value.

Convert the Latin-1 files, preserve footnotes, and keep countries separate from
regional and aggregate rows. This is contextual tourism data for Linz as a
whole—not evidence about Ars Electronica visitors or a causal explanation of
artist representation.
