The nine station endpoints provide varying combinations of particulates, ozone,
temperature, wind, and other measurements. On July 15, five returned live data
and four returned no measurements for the rolling 24-hour period. Use a monitored
server-side adapter, a reviewed station-coordinate registry, and cached
station-level fallbacks; never present the nearest reading as venue-specific
health or safety advice.
