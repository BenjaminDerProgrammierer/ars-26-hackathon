The raw source spans 834 CityGML/DXF tiles, uses EPSG:31255, and includes
textures and multiple files per tile. That is too much weekend setup for most
teams, but selected festival areas can support venue fly-throughs and spatial
storytelling.

This source belongs in a showcase track only after the organizer publishes
corrected venue anchors and web-ready glTF or 3D Tiles. Label it as a 2022 model
whose source imagery dates from 2019–2021; the documented accuracy is about
±2 metres.
