The linked 1876 plan is available in EPSG:4326, EPSG:3857, and EPSG:31255;
the reviewed collection also includes 1945 and other separately catalogued
vintages. These are large whole-city rasters, so organizers should select a few
years and pre-tile or crop festival areas. Use them as visibly dated historical
backdrops, never as current navigation or building/address truth.

Pair the maps with [street names and their meanings](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/807645f0-2e80-4e24-b142-3673b108dde6)
after normalizing festival venue addresses. Keep the original German narratives
alongside any machine-translated text.
