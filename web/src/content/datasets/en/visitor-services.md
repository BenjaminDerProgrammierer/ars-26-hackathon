These sources can support family breaks, hydration stops, Eurokey and
changing-table filters, and nearby accessible parking. The organizer should
publish one normalized WGS84 GeoJSON/CSV bundle: toilets and fountains use
EPSG:31255, accessible parking is a projected Shapefile, and the playground
CSV uses Latin-1 but already contains WGS84 point geometry.

Sources: [public toilets](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/461b3bd7-346d-4401-91d6-8009538c54a1),
[drinking fountains](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/ee7668cf-46bc-4246-a6fa-4adfdb52a513),
[accessible parking](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/9c01fb76-047e-47a9-be70-6e883daa92c1),
and [playgrounds and sports facilities](https://www.data.gv.at/katalog/datasets/b77d0b46-306c-46da-b26d-6e4dd718fde7).
The data is from roughly 2022–2024 and must be presented as a prototype
directory, not a current accessibility, opening-hours, or availability
guarantee.
