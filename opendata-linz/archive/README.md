# Linz Open Data verdict research archive

This archive preserves the dated research and intermediate judgments that led to
the [final dataset overview](../README.md). The top-level dataset folders are the
authoritative verdicts; these documents show how candidates were discovered,
tested, challenged, and narrowed into that portfolio.

The sequence was:

1. a broad title-and-description candidate pass;
2. Benjamin's reproducible inventory of all 820 Stadt Linz catalog records;
3. Rainer's hands-on payload reviews of 25 likely sources;
4. a consolidated festival-specific recommendation, later reflected in the
   top-level verdicts together with 2026-07-15 endpoint and publisher follow-up.

observations. Use it for provenance and decision rationale, not as the current
dataset selection.
