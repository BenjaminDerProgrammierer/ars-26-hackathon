# Baulandreserven 2022

**Final verdict: DO NOT USE until the catalog resource year is fixed.**

**Rainer verdict:** Not reviewed in Rainer's 25-dataset set. Added during the full
820-record catalog audit.

The catalog title says 2022, but its only Atom resource points to
`/baulandreserven/2012/Baulandreserven.shp`. A separate 2012 record also exists,
so consumers may unknowingly use data ten years older than advertised.

The publisher fix is tracked in
[issue #9](https://github.com/BenjaminDerProgrammierer/ars-26-hackathon/issues/9).
Reassess only after the actual vintage and complete distribution are confirmed.
