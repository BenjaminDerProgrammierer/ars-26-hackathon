# Hecken die Schmecken

**Final verdict: OPTIONAL GARNISH.** Do not offer it as a primary dataset.

**Rainer verdict:** ⚠️ Borderline — charming snack-map concept and real Linz park
locations, but no coordinates and four years stale.

The 26 records list berry hedges, named parks/streets, species, and approximate
harvest months. Some September berries make it a playful walking-route addition.

Geocode or manually verify every location and label the 2022 vintage. The publisher
warns that species details may change; do not claim current availability or edible
safety.
