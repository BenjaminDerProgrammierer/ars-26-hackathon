# 3D Geodaten LoD2 2022

**Final verdict: USE WITH PREPARATION FOR A SHOWCASE TRACK.** Preselect and convert
festival-area tiles; the raw citywide source is not weekend-ready.

**Rainer verdict:** ✅ Recommended — real textured 3D building models of Linz,
ideal for festival fly-throughs, but heavy format needs reprojection and tooling.

The source index lists 834 CityGML/DXF tiles with building/terrain data and
textures. It can support a 3D venue explorer or fly-through.

Convert selected EPSG:31255 CityGML tiles to glTF/3D Tiles and anchor them to
corrected festival venue coordinates. Source imagery is from 2019-2021, stated
accuracy is about +/-2 m, and recent buildings may be missing.
