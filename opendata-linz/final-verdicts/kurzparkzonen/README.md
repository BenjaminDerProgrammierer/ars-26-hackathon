# Kurzparkzonen

**Final verdict: OPTIONAL.** Include only if a team has a concrete driving/parking
use case and the organizer has already converted it.

**Rainer verdict:** ⚠️ Borderline — meaningful, spatially joinable data, but ships
only as ESRI Shapefiles in a non-WGS84 projection and has a utility-only thematic
tie.

The polygons can show parking rules near venues, but they are less valuable than
transit and accessibility layers.

Convert the complete Shapefile to WGS84 GeoJSON and label the 2022 vintage. Do not
imply current tariffs or enforcement without publisher verification.
